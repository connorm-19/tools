Services & resupply flags

Day Stage Water Food Notes
D1Le Puy → Montbonnet✅✅Resupply in Le Puy before leaving.
D2Montbonnet → Saugues✅✅Saint-Privat-d'Allier and Monistrol-d'Allier both have cafés. Full services in Saugues.
D3Saugues → Sauvage⚠️⚠️La Chapelle-Saint-Roch (3km past Sauvage) has water. No food beyond Saugues. Sauvage provides dinner.
D4Sauvage → Aumont-Aubrac✅⚠️Saint-Alban (~9km) has café and small grocery. Aumont-Aubrac has a supermarket.
D5Aumont-Aubrac → Nasbinals⚠️❌26km with essentially no services. Les 4 Chemins (~5km) has a café but may be seasonal. Stock food and fill water before leaving AA.
D6Nasbinals → Saint-Côme⚠️⚠️Nasbinals → Saint-Chély (17km): plateau, no services. Aubrac village at 9km has a café. Saint-Chély has a small shop. Stock up at Nasbinals.
D7Saint-Côme → Estaing✅✅Espalion (8km) is a proper town — good lunch stop, bakery, pharmacy.
D8Estaing → Conques✅⚠️Golinhac at ~22km. Limited between Estaing and Golinhac. Conques: tiny village, one épicerie.
D9Conques → Le Terly✅✅Decazeville (~20km) is a proper town. Livinhac-le-Haut at ~24km. Figeac has full services.
D10–D13Rocamadour variant✅⚠️Villages are small. Rocamadour has full tourist services. Stock food at Lacapelle-Marival and Rocamadour.
D14Vers → Lascabannes✅✅Cahors (~17km, midday) has excellent restaurants and a supermarket. Stock up before Lascabannes.
D15–D16Lascabannes → Moissac✅⚠️Lauzerte has a small épicerie. Moissac has full services.
✅ reliable services  ·  ⚠️ limited or seasonal  ·  ❌ none on route
