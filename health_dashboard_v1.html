How this is calculated: Estimated intake = TDEE ± (weight change × 3,500 kcal/lb). TDEE is derived from Oura total burn (resting + activity). Day-over-day weight delta is de-noised using a 5-day rolling median to remove water/glycogen fluctuations. Uncertainty: ±300–500 kcal/day due to scale variability and burn estimate error. No food logs are used — this is entirely back-calculated. Treat as a directional signal, not a precise count.
